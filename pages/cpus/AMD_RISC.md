## 关于 AMD RISC

AMD Am29000 32 位 RISC 微处理器于 1988 年推出，设计用于微型计算机，但主要用作嵌入式处理器。

## Introduction

AMD Am29000 32-bit RISC microprocessor was designed for use in microcomputers, but it was mostly used as an embedded processor.
